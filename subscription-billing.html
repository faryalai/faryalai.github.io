help Frequently Asked Questions
How does billing work? expand_more
All subscriptions are billed monthly through Google Play Billing. Your subscription will automatically renew each month unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.
Can I change plans anytime? expand_more
Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial? expand_more
We offer a 7-day free trial for both Pro and Career Coach plans. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged.
How do I cancel my subscription? expand_more
You can cancel your subscription through the Google Play Store. Go to your account settings in the Play Store, find "Subscriptions," and select the AI Career App subscription to cancel.
